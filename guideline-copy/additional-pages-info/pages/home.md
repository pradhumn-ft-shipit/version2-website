### Advisor Transitions

*Move advisors and books in days not months*

- Automates data gathering from client households
- Seamlessly creates comprehensive household records
- Logically maps data into firm and custodial paperwork
- Supports transitions into prominent custodians

---

### Client Onboarding

*A seamless client journey from the get-go*

- Automates data-gathering from meetings and other communication
- Lets clients confirm pre-filled information
- Auto-populates CRM and financial planning software
- Initiates account opening and delivers disclosures and agreements

---

### Document Intelligence

*From piles of client paperwork to instant insights*

- Extracts data from brokerage/bank/tax/equity comp documents
- Organizes and summarizes information for analysis and reporting
- Enriches insights with current market data
- Exports to your tools (financial planning software, CRM, Excel)

---

### Meeting Assistant

*Your 'always-on' meeting coordinator*

- Generates pre-meeting prep from email and CRM data
- Summarizes in-person and virtual (Zoom, Teams, GMeet) calls
- Drafts contextual follow-up emails and identifies action items
- Updates your CRM and other tools in one click
